Music: Janaya's Form of Cultural Expression

Music is important to me in many ways. It lightens my mood and, most of all, it allows me to connect with my cultural background. I am Dominican and Puerto Rican. I love expressing and appreciating my culture through my music choices. Furthermore, I enjoy a vast array of genres like Salsa, Bachata, and Merengue. These forms of music not only allow me to connect with my culture but also with my family. That is why music is so prevalent in my life.
